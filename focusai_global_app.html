Common Questions

Frequently Answered Queries

Are student videos streamed to the cloud?

No. Real-time visual tracking algorithms execute client-side locally in-memory. Individual facial images are destroyed instantly within milliseconds of evaluation. No personal visual data is ever hosted, streamed, or recorded to external databases.

Does it support Blackboard and Canvas LMS?

Yes, FocusAI is compliant with the standard LTI v1.3 protocol. Instructors can seamlessly link cognitive attention analytics profiles directly into mainstream Learning Management Systems such as Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, and D2L Brightspace.

Is specialized high-performance camera equipment required?

Absolutely not. FocusAI is designed to function smoothly using standard consumer laptops, integrated webcams, or cost-effective CCTV cameras installed in standard university lecture rooms.
